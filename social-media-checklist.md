# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s

- Make sure your profile name and picture look professional

- Take a moment to think before you post something

- Be kind to others and talk to them in a friendly way

- Do not share your personal details to any one keep it private 

- Use social media to learn new things and meet new people

## Don’ts

- Don’t share personal info that you wouldn’t want everyone to see

- Stay away, from posting stuff that’s not suitable or might offend people

- Don’t get into arguments or bully others online

- Be careful and avoid clicking on weird links

- Until news is not confirm do not spread it.
